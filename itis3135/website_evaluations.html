Website Evaluations




TESLA
  • Contrast:

    Tesla's website has very good contrast with the opacity of the images of the background pictures showing the cars and the scenary representation having an opacity that will clearly show the font showing the car names. Also the background pictures showing the cars are light color with the text saying the car models as being a black color text.

  • Repetition: The Repetition on Tesl's website is amazing with it showing the name of the cars with the same font and with each name, associating a picture of the car with 2 other other buttons that link to the custom order and existing inventory.
  • Alignment: The element of objects are aligned in a way that is very organized like a grid and very easy to see and navigate to the specs of the car and also to the purchasing of the car.
  • Proximity: The promity of the webiste icons are very structed to be easy on the eyes with the pictures layed out well and does not strain your eyes.
