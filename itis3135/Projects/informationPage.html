What is sexually transmitted disease?

A Sexually Transmitted Disease also known as an STD, which are also called Sexually transmitted infections are diseases that spread from one person to another through sexual contact. It can be through oral, anal, or vaginal sex for how it is transfered.

Types of STD's

  • HIV/Aids
  • HPV
  • Public Lice
  • Chylmidia
  • Gonorrhea

Symptoms of STD's

  • Fever
  • Abdominal Pain
  • Blisters or Soars in or Around the Mouths
